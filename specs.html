Subsystems Breakdown

Airframe & Structure

Designed for maximum rigidity under high payload strain. The core chassis utilizes a lightweight carbon fiber airframe.

Avionics & Flight Control

The brain of the aircraft is a Pixhawk 6X flight controller running robust open-source flight stack firmware (ArduPilot). It handles real-time stabilization, GPS waypoint navigation, and telemetry communication with our ground control station to ensure autonomous reliability.

Autonomy & Vision Processing

A Raspberry Pi 5 acts as the companion computer, handling computationally intensive tasks. It interfaces directly with a high-resolution downward-facing camera array, running custom computer vision models to identify, classify, and localize ground targets autonomously in real-time.

Power & Delivery Mechanism

Powered by high-capacity LiPo batteries and standalone Electronic Speed Controllers (ESCs) for fail-safe motor operation. The delivery system features a custom-engineered servo release mechanism designed to hold and deploy the payload with pinpoint accuracy over the drop zone.
