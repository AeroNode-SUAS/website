Who we are

AeroNode is a student-led team of engineers, programmers, and aviation enthusiasts dedicated to designing and building an autonomous aircraft for the SUAS competition. Our mission is to push the boundaries of what students can achieve in aerospace technology.

Founded in 2026, we are based in Bellevue, Washington, and our team is composed of passionate students from various disciplines. We believe in hands-on learning, innovation, and collaboration to create a competitive autonomous aircraft that can perform complex tasks in real-world scenarios.
